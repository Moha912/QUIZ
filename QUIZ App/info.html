Rules of the game: Quiz

The game consists of:

45 cards in 4 categories.

4 part in 4 categories.

-“Easy” questions, value: 1 point (1 Star).

-“Somewhat difficult” questions, value: 2 points (2 Star).

-“Difficult” questions, value: 3 points (3 Star).

-1 very, very difficult red card called, which returns 4 points (4 Star).

The player is invited to answer it. If he answers correctly, he collects the number of points corresponding to the value of the card.

If his answer is wrong or if he cannot answer the question, it is the Master of the game that collects the points!.. WARNING, this rule is valid for all cards.

The first player (or Master of the game) who will capitalize 10 points will have won the game. Players are free to choose the value of the questions they want to answer to form their ball of points; the more difficult the choice of the question the more points it yields...
